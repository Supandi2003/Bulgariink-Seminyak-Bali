About Studio

Welcome to Bulgari Ink Seminyak — where art meets identity. We are a professional tattoo studio based in Seminyak, Bali, specializing in custom designs, realism, and blackwork, crafted for both local and international clients.

Since 2016, we have been dedicated to creating tattoos with meaning, precision, and exceptional attention to detail. Our experienced artists work closely with you to transform your ideas into timeless artwork you’ll carry with pride for a lifetime.

We prioritize hygiene, safety, and comfort at every step, using only high-quality equipment and maintaining the highest industry standards to ensure a clean and professional experience.

More than just a tattoo, this is your story — your identity expressed through art.
